# ContinuityM — Audio Essay Collection

This repository contains high-quality audio editions of the ContinuityM essays, exploring the future of intelligence, digital migration, planetary computation, and the long-term evolution of technological civilization.

The recordings are divided into two downloadable packs for convenience.

---

## **Pack 1 — Essays 1–5**
1. **The Architecture of Self-Modeling Systems**  
2. **Digital Human Migration**  
3. **The Computational Earth**  
4. **The Computational Solar System**  
5. **Continuity and the Structure of Reality**

These essays introduce the core conceptual framework:  
how intelligence expands, how continuity emerges, and how computation reshapes planetary and interplanetary environments.

---

## **Pack 2 — Essays 6–10**
6. **The Technical Foundations of Digital Migration**  
7. **The Architecture of Computational Earth**  
8. **The Emergence of Interplanetary Identity**  
9. **Humanoid Robots and the Future of Computation**  
10. **Biological Humanity in a Machine Civilization**

These essays examine the transitional mechanisms, infrastructural systems, and cultural transformations that enable digital civilization to extend beyond biology and across the solar system.
